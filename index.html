Projects

Modern Mobile Banking App

A mobile app designed for secure and intuitive banking on the go, focusing on simplicity and trust.

Problem

Traditional banking apps often overwhelm users with complex menus and security jargon, leading to frustration and low adoption among younger demographics.

Solution

Developed a clean, minimalist interface with guided onboarding, biometric authentication, and simplified transaction flows to build user confidence.

Target Users

Young professionals and millennials aged 18-35 who prioritize quick, secure access to banking services without unnecessary complexity.

Design Process

Conducted user interviews to identify pain points, created user personas, sketched wireframes in Figma, prototyped interactions, and iterated based on usability testing feedback.

Outcome

The app concept received positive feedback in mock tests, with users completing transactions 30% faster compared to existing apps, emphasizing ease of use and security.

Grocery Delivery Mobile App

An app that streamlines online grocery shopping with personalized recommendations and seamless checkout.

Problem

Users struggle with cluttered grocery apps that make finding items and managing carts tedious, often leading to abandoned carts.

Solution

Implemented a voice search feature, smart categorization, and a one-tap reorder system to enhance efficiency and user satisfaction.

Target Users

Busy families and individuals aged 25-50 who value convenience and time-saving features in their daily shopping.

Design Process

Analyzed competitor apps, defined user journeys, created low-fidelity wireframes, built interactive prototypes in Figma, and refined designs through A/B testing.

Outcome

Prototypes showed a 25% increase in user engagement, with features like voice search reducing search time by 40%, making the app more accessible and enjoyable.

Healthcare Web Platform

A web platform for patients to manage health records, schedule appointments, and communicate with providers securely.

Problem

Patients often face difficulties navigating healthcare portals, leading to missed appointments and poor communication with doctors.

Solution

Designed an accessible dashboard with clear navigation, telemedicine integration, and automated reminders to improve patient engagement.

Target Users

Patients of all ages, with a focus on elderly users and those with chronic conditions who need reliable, easy-to-use health management tools.

Design Process

Conducted accessibility audits, developed user personas, mapped out user flows, created responsive wireframes, and tested for usability with diverse user groups.

Outcome

The platform concept improved user satisfaction scores by 35% in simulations, with features ensuring compliance with accessibility standards and reducing no-show rates.

Travel Planning Mobile App

An app for planning trips with collaborative features, budget tracking, and real-time updates.

Problem

Travel planning apps lack personalization and collaboration, causing stress and inefficiencies for group travelers.

Solution

Built a gamified planning interface with shared itineraries, AI-driven suggestions, and integrated booking to make travel fun and organized.

Target Users

Adventure seekers and families aged 20-45 who plan trips collaboratively and seek tailored experiences.

Design Process

Gathered insights from travel forums, crafted user stories, designed mood boards, prototyped in Adobe XD, and validated with user feedback loops.

Outcome

User testing indicated a 40% reduction in planning time, with collaborative features fostering better group experiences and higher app retention.
